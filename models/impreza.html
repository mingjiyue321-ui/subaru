Subaru Impreza

The Impreza is Subaru's entry-level car, but it's anything but basic. It's the least expensive vehicle in North America to offer standard all-wheel drive. Available as a 5-door hatchback or 4-door sedan, the Impreza provides confident traction in rain or snow. The 2.0L Boxer engine (152 hp) prioritizes efficiency (28 city / 36 highway) and delivers a smooth, composed ride.

Inside, the Impreza surprises with a spacious cabin, Apple CarPlay/Android Auto, and available EyeSight safety tech. The hatchback offers 56 cu ft of cargo space with rear seats folded – more than some small SUVs. While it doesn't have the WRX's turbo power, the Impreza is an ideal first car for young drivers or a practical commuter for anyone who faces harsh winters. And like all Subarus, it holds its value remarkably well.
