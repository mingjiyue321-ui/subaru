Subaru Forester

The Subaru Forester is the ultimate compact SUV for families who love the outdoors. With its standard Symmetrical All-Wheel Drive and 8.7 inches of ground clearance, it handles mud, snow, and gravel roads with ease. The boxer engine lowers the center of gravity, giving you confident handling even on winding mountain passes.

Inside, the Forester offers best-in-class visibility thanks to large windows and a low dashboard. The rear seats fold flat for camping gear or a weekend's worth of hiking equipment. Every Forester comes with EyeSight Driver Assist Technology – including pre-collision braking and adaptive cruise control. For 2026, the Wilderness trim adds even more rugged touches: all-terrain tires, a front skid plate, and a raised roof rail system.

Key Specs (estimated)

  • Engine: 2.5L 4-cylinder Boxer (182 hp)
  • MPG: 26 city / 33 highway
  • Cargo space: 76.1 cu ft rear seats down
  • Safety: IIHS Top Safety Pick+
