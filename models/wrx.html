Subaru WRX

The WRX carries Subaru's World Rally Championship DNA directly to the street. Under the hood sits a turbocharged 2.4L Boxer engine making 271 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, paired with either a six-speed manual or a CVT with simulated shifts. The Symmetrical All-Wheel Drive system is specially tuned for performance – you can get power to the ground even in rain or loose gravel.

Exterior styling is aggressive: functional air vents, a hood scoop, flared fenders, and a large rear diffuser. Inside, the WRX adds sport seats, aluminum pedals, and a 11.6-inch touchscreen. For hardcore enthusiasts, the WRX GT trim offers electronically controlled dampers and a Drive Mode Select with a dedicated track setting. The WRX is not just a car – it's a community, with a massive aftermarket and active club racing scene.

Performance

  • 271 hp / 258 lb-ft
  • 0-60 mph: 5.5 sec (manual)
  • Standard AWD with torque vectoring
  • Brembo brakes (GT trim)
