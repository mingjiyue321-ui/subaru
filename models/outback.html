Subaru Outback

The Outback is Subaru's iconic crossover wagon – a vehicle that blurred the line between station wagon and SUV. With 8.7 inches of ground clearance (9.5 inches on Wilderness), it feels as capable as many traditional trucks, yet drives like a car. The long roof gives you over 75 cubic feet of cargo space, perfect for skis, bikes, or a week's worth of camping supplies.

Under the hood, you can choose the standard 2.5L Boxer (182 hp) or the punchy 2.4L turbo (260 hp) that makes highway merging effortless. The Outback also features dual-screen infotainment (standard 11.6-inch) and Subaru's latest EyeSight with wide-angle camera. For off-road lovers, the Wilderness edition adds all-terrain tires, a front skid plate, and a roof basket. It's no surprise that Outback owners are among the most loyal in the industry.

Key Specs

  • Engine: 2.5L or 2.4L Turbo Boxer
  • MPG: 26 city / 33 highway (base)
  • Towing: Up to 3,500 lbs (turbo)
  • Approach angle: 20.0° (Wilderness: 20.8°)
