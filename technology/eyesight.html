EyeSight Driver Assist

EyeSight is Subaru's proprietary suite of active safety features, using a pair of full‑color stereo cameras mounted behind the windshield – mimicking human binocular vision. The system can recognize vehicles, pedestrians, cyclists, and even road markings. It operates at speeds up to 90 mph and functions in a wide range of lighting conditions.

Key functions include Pre‑collision Braking (warns and automatically brakes if a collision is imminent), Adaptive Cruise Control (maintains safe distance, stops and restarts in traffic), Lane Keep Assist (gently steers to keep you centered), and Lead Vehicle Start Alert (notifies you when traffic moves forward). EyeSight has helped reduce rear‑end crashes by up to 85% in real‑world driving. As of 2026, EyeSight is standard on every automatic Subaru model.

EyeSight Generation 4 (2024+)

  • Wider angle: 160° field of view
  • Improved low‑light performance
  • Emergency steering assist (avoids obstacles)
  • Blind spot detection integrated with cameras
